About This Initiative

Western University is launching the "Share Your Story" initiative to celebrate the diverse journeys, achievements, and lived experiences of our alumni community. Your story matters—and it has the power to inspire current students and fellow graduates alike.

What We're Looking For

Alumni are invited to reflect on their time at Western and share:

  • The mentors who shaped your journey
  • Challenges you overcame during and after university
  • Personal milestones and professional accomplishments
  • How Western contributed to your growth beyond the classroom

Your Impact

Selected stories will be featured across Western's digital platforms, helping to build a richer, more connected alumni network. By sharing your journey, you contribute to a legacy of Purple-and-White pride and remind the next generation that every successful path begins with a single step—and a supportive community behind it.
